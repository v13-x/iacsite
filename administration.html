Principal & Academic Coordinator for Administration & Stakeholder Support

Mr. Paolo Antonio Noceda serves as the Senior High School Principal and is responsible for the overall leadership and direction of the SHS department. His role involves ensuring the successful implementation of school programs and policies, promoting academic excellence, overseeing faculty performance, and safeguarding student welfare. Under his guidance, the SHS department strives to maintain high standards in both education and operations. Mr. Paolo Domingo serves as the Academic Coordinator for Administration and Stakeholder Support. He manages the administrative backbone of the SHS department, including class schedules, documentation, and internal logistics. In addition, he fosters communication and collaboration between the school and its stakeholders, including parents and external partners, and supports student activities and community engagement efforts.

Academic Coordinator for Teacher Management & Development

Mr. Wilson Tiu holds the position of Academic Coordinator for Teacher Management and Development. He is primarily in charge of supporting and enhancing teacher performance through evaluations, professional development programs, and instructional quality checks. Mr. Tiu plays a vital role in ensuring that faculty members receive the training and guidance they need to grow in their teaching careers and deliver effective education.

Academic Coordinator for Curriculum & Institutional Support

Ms. Fara Portia Alvendia is the Academic Coordinator for Curriculum and Institutional Support. Her work revolves around managing the academic curriculum, including planning, revising, and aligning instructional materials with institutional and DepEd standards. She provides essential academic resources to faculty and helps maintain the integrity and relevance of the school's learning outcomes.
